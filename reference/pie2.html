JumpShoot: Rise of the Big Chungus.

Description:

The objective is to make a two-player jumping game using java. There will be multiple platforms that players can jump on to avoid being brutally shot by one another. The player's job is to not be a failure and lose all three of their lives. Whoever survives is the winner.

Player Movement

Movement will be controlled by 4 keys (e.g. the WASD keys). The up key will cause the player to jump. Jumping is currently only allowed if the player is on a platform, however we hope to add double jumping as a feature. Longer key presses of the jump key will result in higher jumps. The left and right keys will cause the player to move in that direction at a constant speed while they are pressed down, even if the player is in the air. The down key will cause the player to accelerate downwards faster than normal.

Shooting

There will be one or multiple balls in play. The balls can be in two different states, dangerous and pickupable. The default state of a ball is pickupable. If a ball is pickupable, a player can press a key to pick it up. Players can only hold one ball at a time. If a player has a ball, they can shoot/throw it. A ball is dangerous after a player throws it until it hits a wall. It then becomes pickupable. If a player comes into contact with a dangerous ball, they lose a life. A player dies when all their lives are gone.

Fun Stuff

We anticipate making the balls look like rabbit (Chungus) heads.

Main Objectives:

1. Kill
2. Don't be killed
3. Win
